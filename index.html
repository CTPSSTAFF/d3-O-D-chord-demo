Origin / Destination Highway Flows in the Boston Region MPO Area

This interactive graphic shows origin / destination highway traffic flow patterns among the districts in the Boston Region MPO.

Select a district by mousing over the light-colored edge of the circle. Then mouse-over a link to see the number of daily highway trips between the selected district and another district. Thicker links mean more daily highway trips.

Districts are linked only if there were at least 5,000 daily highway trips between them. If a district does not appear in the graphic, it is because there were fewer than 5,000 daily highway trips between it and any other district in 2012.

Source: CTPS.
